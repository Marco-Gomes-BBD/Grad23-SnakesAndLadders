1. Each player enters their player name and selects a hue.
  2. Players cannot select the same hue or have the same name.
  3. Press Proceed to go to the game.
  4. Each player's icon will be placed on the space that says 'start'.
  5. Take it in turns to roll the dice.
  6. When it is your turn you will be prompted to roll the dice.
  7. Your icon will be moved forward the number of spaces shown on the dice.
  8. If your counter lands at the bottom of a ladder, you will move up to the top of the ladder.
  9. If your counter lands on the head of a snake, you must slide down to the bottom of the snake.
  10. The first player to get to the space that says 'home' is the winner.
